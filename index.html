What is Wordle?

Wordle is a fun and simple word puzzle game where your goal is to guess a hidden 5-letter word in six attempts or fewer. Here's how to play:

Steps to Play Wordle:

Start with a Guess: Enter any 5-letter word as your first guess. The word doesn't need to be related to the hidden word, but it's often helpful to start with a word that uses common vowels (like "audio" or "table") and consonants (like "stone" or "plant").


Analyze the Feedback: After you submit your guess, the game will give you feedback using colors:

Green: The letter is correct and in the right position.

Yellow: The letter is correct but in the wrong position.

Gray: The letter is not in the word at all.

This feedback helps you narrow down the possibilities for the hidden word.

Make Your Next Guess: Use the feedback from the previous guess to inform your next one. Avoid using gray letters, and try to rearrange yellow letters while keeping the green ones in place.

Keep Guessing: Continue making guesses based on the feedback until you either.


Correctly guess the word (all letters will be green).

Tips for Playing:


Start with a word that includes common vowels and consonants to maximize the information you get from your first guess.

Once you've identified some correct letters, focus on guessing their correct positions.

If a letter is marked yellow, try it in different positions in subsequent guesses.

If a letter is gray, avoid it in future guesses.

That's it! It's a game of deduction, strategy, and a little bit of luck. Good luck with your Wordle games!
